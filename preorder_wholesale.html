STEP 1: Prepare Sales products by import/export functions

  1. Export your products and open the CSV file. The key columns you will edit are Handle and Tags.

  2. Update Handle to a unique value for Sales items (example: womens-urban-shorts ➡️ womens-urban-shorts-sales) to avoid overwriting the original product.

  3. Update Tags to include one dedicated Sales tag (example: Sales). You will reuse this tag in the next steps.

STEP 2: Configure to exclude products from being searched

  1. Follow the guide here: Exclude products from Search.

  2. Add the same Sales tag from STEP 1 to the theme search exclusion settings.

STEP 3: Create secret smart collection

  1. Use the guide here: Protected Collection to create a secret collection.

  2. Configure the smart condition to include all Sales products: Tag is equal to the Sales tag from STEP 1.

STEP 4: Ensure Sales products are not imported to other collections

  1. Go to other collections that use smart conditions.

  2. Add an exclusion rule so Sales products are filtered out: Tag is not equal to the Sales tag from STEP 1.
